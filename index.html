about glorious childcare

Welcome to Glorious Childcare, we are a child care provider that gives peace of mind while you're away from your child. We tailor our childcare and educational services to promote consistent steady growth for each child we serve. We believe that partnering with parents and families in early childhood education will stimulate a love for learning in all subject domains. We are open from 6:30am to 6:30pm from Monday to Friday. Our preschool offers nutritious meals each day. Morning Snack, Lunch, Afternoon Snack and Late Snack will be offered daily.
